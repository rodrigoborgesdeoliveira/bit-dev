# Your first component-driven wiki

<Subtitle>Welcome to your component-driven wiki app!</Subtitle>

This is the first **component-driven wiki!** Content pages are MDX components, and you can use any type of component
from inside any wiki page.

Here is an example using our [What is Bit](https://bit.cloud/teambit/bit/content/what-is-bit) component:

<br />
<br />

<BubbleHighlighter
  componentId={ComponentID.fromString('teambit.bit/content/what-is-bit')}
  showId
>
  <WhatIsBit size={Size.SMALL} />
</BubbleHighlighter>
